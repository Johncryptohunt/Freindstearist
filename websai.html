About San cai

Hello, I'm Sancai V. Magdaraog, a 21-year-old student at EARIST Cavite pursuing architectural drafting.

Aside from that, I love music, and without it, my life would be complete chaos because I have to choose it each time I need a break from the demanding outside world.
